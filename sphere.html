The Five SPHERE Dimensions

Confidentiality
Controls who can access information. Traditional CIA element focused on preventing unauthorized disclosure.
Integrity
Ensures data remains unaltered. Traditional CIA element protecting against unauthorized modification.
Availability
Guarantees authorized access when needed. Traditional CIA element ensuring systems remain operational.
Authenticity ELEVATED
Did this asset come from the approved source, and has its custody been unbroken? Critical for detecting AI data poisoning and supply chain attacks that integrity checks alone cannot catch.
Non-Repudiation ELEVATED
Is this data truthful or legitimate, and can we prove the actor's liability for its creation? Essential for accountability in automated systems and AI-driven threats.
